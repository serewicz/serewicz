# Case Study: Regulated FinTech Platform

## Scenario

A regulated FinTech platform is preparing for investor review and board-level technology governance discussion. The company operates in a compliance-heavy environment with sensitive customer data and third-party dependencies.

## Problem

The review identifies compliance readiness gaps, vendor concentration, privileged access concerns, incident reporting maturity questions, CRA readiness needs, and AI governance risk.

These issues create business exposure because regulated platforms need clear controls, evidence, ownership, and escalation paths.

## Repositories Used

| Repository | Role |
|---|---|
| [Executive AI Advisor](https://github.com/serewicz/Executive-AI-Advisor) | Generates CRA readiness, AI governance, diligence, and security-risk outputs. |
| [CTO Operating System](https://github.com/serewicz/cto-operating-system) | Provides board reporting, governance templates, and risk register structure. |
| [K8s Platform Blueprint](https://github.com/serewicz/k8s-platform-blueprint) | Provides policy-as-code, access control, observability, and compliance evidence patterns. |
| [Engineering Operating Metrics](https://github.com/serewicz/engineering-operating-metrics) | Measures review quality, risk controls, rework, and governance exceptions. |

## Operating Workflow

1. Use Executive AI Advisor to generate CRA readiness and AI governance assessments from company documents.
2. Structure management questions and board discussion points with CTO Operating System templates.
3. Map platform and access-control findings to K8s Platform Blueprint remediation patterns.
4. Track engineering review quality and risk-control trends with Engineering Operating Metrics.

## Outputs

- CRA Readiness Assessment
- AI Governance Assessment
- Security Risk Summary
- Board Discussion Points

## Example Board Questions

- Which obligations require legal confirmation before product or market expansion?
- Are privileged access paths reviewed and evidenced?
- Which vendors create material continuity or compliance risk?
- How are AI use cases governed, reviewed, and monitored?
- What incident reporting gaps need immediate remediation?

## Business Value

The case study shows how regulated technology risk can be translated into board-ready governance language, implementation controls, and measurable operating improvements.
