# Case Study: Growth Equity B2B SaaS

## Scenario

A growth-stage B2B SaaS company is preparing for growth equity investment. Revenue is expanding, but scaling pressure is exposing weaknesses in cloud cost visibility, delivery predictability, security governance, and AI readiness.

## Problem

The company needs to demonstrate that technology can support continued growth without margin erosion, operational instability, or unmanaged AI risk.

Key issues include cloud cost growth, inconsistent delivery cadence, incomplete security governance, unclear platform ownership, and early AI usage without formal governance.

## Repositories Used

| Repository | Role |
|---|---|
| [Executive AI Advisor](https://github.com/serewicz/Executive-AI-Advisor) | Performs growth-equity diligence and creates cited executive outputs. |
| [CTO Operating System](https://github.com/serewicz/cto-operating-system) | Defines operating cadence, board reporting, and technology planning framework. |
| [K8s Platform Blueprint](https://github.com/serewicz/k8s-platform-blueprint) | Provides FinOps, platform governance, observability, and Kubernetes control patterns. |
| [Engineering Operating Metrics](https://github.com/serewicz/engineering-operating-metrics) | Tracks delivery flow, review quality, rework, cost, risk, and governance. |

## Operating Workflow

1. Use Executive AI Advisor to analyze the diligence package.
2. Generate a Board Technology Brief and growth-equity 100-day plan.
3. Use CTO Operating System to define monthly operating cadence and board reporting.
4. Apply K8s Platform Blueprint patterns for cost allocation, policy controls, and platform governance.
5. Use Engineering Operating Metrics to track delivery predictability and quality trends.

## Outputs

- Board Technology Brief
- Growth Equity 100-Day Plan
- Cloud Cost Review
- Engineering Operating Dashboard

## Example Board Questions

- Is cloud spend growing faster than customer or revenue growth?
- Which platform controls are needed before the next scaling phase?
- Are delivery delays caused by process, architecture, staffing, or quality issues?
- What AI governance is required before broader adoption?

## Business Value

The case study shows how a scaling company can convert diligence into a growth operating plan. The focus is not just identifying risks, but creating a cadence for cost control, platform governance, delivery improvement, and board communication.
